Oracle Intelligent Bots Advanced Training - Lab 8a (User Experience)

In the preceding presentation you have heard about ideas for bringing a positive user experience (UX) to your bot. In this lab, we'll break into groups to discuss ideas for creating a more positive flower buying experience.

Ideas for Positive UX

With this lab, we'll step away from hands-on tasks for a bit.

Within small working groups that your instructor will set up, we'll reflect upon the bot we have created so far and discuss (and document) ways that we can create a better user experience for the bot.

Consider existing features or language of the bot you would change. And also consider new features you would suggest adding. Your ideas might be stimulated, but not limited to the following discussion points:

  1. The general tone and style of language the bot is using.
  2. The personality (or lack of) of the bot – should it have name?
  3. The general process of buying flowers using this bot.
  4. How the bot guides and helps the user if they are unsure of what to do.
  5. How the bot deals with user error or not understanding the user.
  6. Should the bot interaction be different based on a specific context within the process?
  7. What recommendations of new features might you make to the business stakeholders?
  8. What smalltalk topics do you think you might have to handle?

Remember, you don't have to implement these features, but you should be thinking about the UX goals you would like to achieve.

After this discussion, each group will be asked to nominate a spokesperson who will present your findings.
